Welcome to a simplified version of Yahtzee. Amazon's #1 best-selling dice game.

If you're not familiar with the game, check out the rules here.

We have simplified the Yahtzee Game by adopting the below changes:

  • There will be 6 rounds or turns, each comprised of three dice rolls
  • Score combinations will be only from the lower section
  • No bonus scores of any kind

Otherwise, the rules are the same as in the above-linked article. The green boxes are selected dice. You can toggle the selection by clicking on them. On the first roll of every round you need to roll all the five dice, so you won't be able to toggle until after that first roll. Let's get rolling!
