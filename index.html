Welcome to a simplified version of Yahtzee. Amazon's #1 best-selling dice game.

Before continuing please read this article to understand the Yahtzee Game.

We have simplified the Yahtzee Game by adopting the below changes:

  • There will be 6 rounds (or 6 turns, each comprising of three dice rolls)
  • Score combinations will be only from the lower-section
  • No bonus scores of any kind

Rest all the rules remain same as stated in the above-linked article. The green boxes are selected dice. You can toggle the selection by clicking on them. Remember, on first roll of every round you need to roll all the five dices so toggle will be off for first roll of every round. Let's get rolling!
