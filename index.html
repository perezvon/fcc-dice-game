Welcome to the Dice Game!

Gameplay

  • You have three rolls maximum.
  • On the first roll, you roll all the dice, and on subsequent rolls you can choose to roll some or all of the dice again.

Scoring

  • 5 of a Kind: 50 points
  • Large Straight (5 consecutive numbers): 40 points
  • Small Straight (4 consecutive numbers): 30 points
  • Full House (three of one number, two of another): 25 points
  • Other: sum of dice
